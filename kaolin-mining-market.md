<p><p>Kaolin mining market is divided into various types based on the processing techniques used. Water-washed kaolin is produced by washing the ore to remove impurities. Airfloat kaolin is produced by air classification to achieve a fine particle size. Calcined kaolin is heated to remove water and impurities. Delaminated kaolin undergoes a refinement process to separate layers. Surface-modified kaolin is coated with chemicals to enhance its properties. Unprocessed kaolin is raw material that has not undergone any processing.</p></p>
