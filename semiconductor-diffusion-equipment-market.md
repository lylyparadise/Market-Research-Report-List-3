<p><p>Semiconductor Diffusion Equipment market includes various types of furnaces such as Oxidation Furnace, Annealing Furnace, and RTP Furnace. Oxidation Furnaces are used for growing oxide layers on wafers, Annealing Furnaces are used for heat treating the wafers to activate dopants, while RTP (Rapid Thermal Processing) Furnaces are used for quickly heating and cooling the wafers to anneal them. These different types of furnaces cater to the specific needs of the semiconductor industry in the process of diffusion.</p></p>
