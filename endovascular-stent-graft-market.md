<p><p>Endovascular stent graft market includes various types such as synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic stent grafts. Synthetic stent grafts are made from materials like polyester or polytetrafluoroethylene, offering durability and flexibility. Biological stent grafts are derived from animal tissues, promoting natural healing and tissue integration. Biosynthetic stent grafts combine synthetic and biological materials, aiming to provide the benefits of both types. Each type has its own strengths and limitations, catering to different patient needs and preferences in the endovascular treatment of aortic aneurysms.</p></p>
