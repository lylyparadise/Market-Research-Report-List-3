<p><p>The Bike Headlights market is divided into two main types: Self Generating Headlights and Battery Powered Headlights. Self Generating Headlights use the motion of the bicycle to generate power, eliminating the need for batteries and reducing maintenance costs. On the other hand, Battery Powered Headlights rely on rechargeable or disposable batteries for power. While Self Generating Headlights are considered more eco-friendly and cost-effective in the long run, Battery Powered Headlights offer the convenience of easily replaceable power sources. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of cyclists.</p></p>
