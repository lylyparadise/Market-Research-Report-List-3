<p><strong>The Air-to-Air Refueling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MPRS</li><li>Wing-to-Wing Refueling</li><li>Simple Grappling Refueling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air-to-air refueling market includes three main types of refueling techniques: MPRS (Multifunctional Probe and Drogue Refueling System), Wing-to-Wing Refueling, and Simple Grappling Refueling. MPRS involves a probe on the receiving aircraft which connects to a drogue on the tanker aircraft. Wing-to-wing refueling involves a boom extending from the tanker to connect with a receptacle on the receiving aircraft's wing. Simple grappling refueling uses a hose and drogue system where the receiver aircraft flies behind the tanker to receive fuel.</p></p>
