<p><p>Paper cushion systems are versatile packaging solutions that offer protection and cushioning for fragile items during transportation. There are three main types of paper cushion systems: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual systems require operators to manually dispense and form paper cushioning, while semi-automatic systems involve some level of automation in the dispensing process. Automatic systems are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. Each type caters to different production volumes and packaging needs, providing varying levels of efficiency and convenience.</p></p>
