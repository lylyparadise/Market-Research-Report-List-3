<p><strong>The Audio Frequency Oscillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>R-C Oscillator</li><li>L-C Oscillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio frequency oscillators are electronic devices used to generate sine waves with frequencies in the audio range. The two main types of audio frequency oscillators are R-C oscillators and L-C oscillators. R-C oscillators use resistors and capacitors to control the frequency of the output signal, while L-C oscillators use inductors and capacitors for frequency control. Both types of oscillators are widely used in audio equipment such as synthesizers, tone generators, and audio test equipment.</p></p>
