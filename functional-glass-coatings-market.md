<p><ul><li>Pyrolytic Coating</li><li>Sputtered Coating</li><li>Screen Coating</li><li>Spray Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional glass coatings market can be categorized into various types such as pyrolytic coating, sputtered coating, screen coating, spray coating, and others. Pyrolytic coating involves applying a thin film of coating on the glass through a high-temperature process. Sputtered coating is done by depositing thin layers of coating on the glass using a vacuum technique. Screen coating involves applying coating using a screen printing process. Spray coating is done by spraying a liquid coating onto the glass surface. Other types include innovative and specialized coating methods for specific applications.</p></p>
