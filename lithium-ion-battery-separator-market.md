<p><strong>The Lithium-Ion Battery Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Layer (PP/PE)</li><li>Bilayer (PP/PE, PP/PP)</li><li>Trilayer (PP/PE/PP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithium-ion battery separators are available in three main types: Single-layer (PP/PE), Bilayer (PP/PE, PP/PP), and Trilayer (PP/PE/PP). Single-layer separators are made from a single material, either polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE). Bilayer separators consist of two layers, typically PP and PE or two layers of PP. Trilayer separators have three layers, with PP on the outside and PE in the middle. These different types offer varying levels of performance and stability in lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
