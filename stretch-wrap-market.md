<p><p>Stretch wrap market can be divided into two main types: Manual stretch wrap and Machine stretch wrap. Manual stretch wrap is a hand-held film wrapping solution that requires manual labor to wrap or secure pallets and products. On the other hand, Machine stretch wrap is a more automated solution that utilizes machines to wrap pallets and products with stretch film. While manual stretch wrap is suitable for small-scale operations and provides flexibility, machine stretch wrap is more efficient and cost-effective for larger-scale operations.</p></p>
