<p><p>Metallized papers can be classified into two main types based on the method of metallization: lamination type and vacuum metallization type. Lamination type metallized papers involve applying a thin layer of metal to the paper through a lamination process, which provides a glossy and reflective surface. On the other hand, vacuum metallization type metallized papers involve vaporizing metal in a vacuum chamber and depositing it onto the paper, creating a more uniform and durable metal coating. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
