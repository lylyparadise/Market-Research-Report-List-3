<p><p>Additives for floor coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of the coating. Anti-foaming additives help prevent the formation of foam during application, while wetting and dispersion additives improve the coating's spreadability and adhesion. Biocidal additives protect against microbial growth, and rheology modification additives control the coating's viscosity. Impact modification additives increase durability and resistance to damage. Other additives may include UV stabilizers, colorants, or fillers to further enhance the coating's properties.</p></p>
