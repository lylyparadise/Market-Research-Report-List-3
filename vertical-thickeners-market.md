<p><p>Vertical thickeners are used in wastewater treatment for solids separation. Two common types are Vertical Gravity Thickeners and Vertical Screw Thickeners. Vertical Gravity Thickeners rely on gravity to separate solids from liquid, while Vertical Screw Thickeners use a rotating screw to achieve the same goal. Both types are efficient in thickening sludge and reducing the volume of wastewater, making them popular choices in water treatment plants and industrial facilities.</p></p>
