There are two main types of defibrillators: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically implanted in the chest, while External Defibrillators (EDs) are portable devices used in emergency situations outside of the hospital. Both types of defibrillators play a crucial role in saving lives by quickly delivering lifesaving shocks to the heart when irregular heartbeats occur.</p></p>
