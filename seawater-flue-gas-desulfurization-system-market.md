<p><p>Seawater Flue Gas Desulfurization System market offers three main types: Wet FGD System, Dry FGD System, and Semi-Dry FGD System. Wet FGD System involves spraying seawater or a water-based solution onto the flue gas to remove sulfur dioxide. Dry FGD System uses a dry sorbent material to capture sulfur dioxide from the flue gas. Semi-Dry FGD System combines elements of both wet and dry systems, offering a more cost-effective and environmentally friendly solution for desulfurization in seawater environments.</p></p>
