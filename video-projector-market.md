<p><p>Video projectors are available in different market types based on their connectivity options. DisplayPort projectors allow for high-quality video and audio output. MHL projectors support smartphones and tablets with MHL compatibility. USB Video projectors allow for direct playback of videos from a USB drive. Wireless projectors enable wireless screen mirroring and streaming from compatible devices. Each market type caters to different user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
