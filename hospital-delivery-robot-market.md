<p><p>Hospital delivery robots can be classified into two main types based on their navigation systems: Magnet Navigation and Laser Navigation. </p><p>Magnet Navigation robots use magnetic strips placed on the floor to create a predetermined pathway for the robot to follow. This navigation method is simple and cost-effective, making it ideal for hospitals on a budget.</p><p>On the other hand, Laser Navigation robots use lasers to map their surroundings in real-time and navigate autonomously. This technology offers greater flexibility and adaptability in navigating complex hospital environments.</p></p>
