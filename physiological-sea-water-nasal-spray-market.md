<p><p>Physiological sea water nasal sprays are categorized into two main types based on their salt concentration levels: isotonic and hypertonic. Isotonic physiological sea water nasal sprays have a salt concentration equivalent to that of the body's cells, making them gentle and suitable for everyday use to maintain nasal hygiene and alleviate congestion. On the other hand, hypertonic physiological sea water nasal sprays have a higher salt concentration, making them more effective in reducing nasal congestion and inflammation, often recommended for treating colds or sinus infections.</p></p>
