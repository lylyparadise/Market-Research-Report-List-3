<p><p>Aircraft windshield wiper and washer systems can be classified into two markets: line fit and retrofit. Line fit refers to the installation of these systems by the aircraft manufacturer during the production of the aircraft. Retrofit, on the other hand, involves the installation of these systems after the aircraft has been delivered to the operator. Line fit systems are typically more integrated and customized, while retrofit systems may be more standardized and compatible with a wider range of aircraft models.</p></p>
