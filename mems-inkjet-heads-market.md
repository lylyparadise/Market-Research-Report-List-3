<p><p>MEMS inkjet heads can be categorized into two main types - continuous inkjet and random inkjet. Continuous inkjet heads are typically used in applications where a continuous stream of ink droplets is needed, such as in continuous printing or marking. On the other hand, random inkjet heads are used when precise placement of individual ink droplets is required, such as in the production of small electronic components or patterns. These two market types cater to different industries and applications, providing a wide range of solutions for various printing needs.</p></p>
