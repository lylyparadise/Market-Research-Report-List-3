<p><p>Reverse jet filters are commonly split into two main market types: Cartridge Type Pulse Jet Filters and Bag Type Pulse Jet Filters. Cartridge Type Pulse Jet Filters utilize a series of cartridge filters to capture dust particles, while Bag Type Pulse Jet Filters use bags made of woven or felted fabric. Both types operate by using a reverse air flow to clean the filters periodically and remove accumulated dust, making them efficient solutions for industrial air filtration needs.</p></p>
