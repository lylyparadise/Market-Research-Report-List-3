<p><p>Special type seed coating agents are utilized in various forms in the market. Suspended agents are dry powders mixed with water before application. Emulsions are liquids composed of oil and water that require agitation for use. Wettable powders are finely ground particles that dissolve in water easily. Other types include granules and pellets that can be applied directly to seeds. These different forms offer flexibility for farmers to choose the most suitable method for their specific crop and conditions.</p></p>
