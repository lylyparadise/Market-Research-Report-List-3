<p><p>Thyristor power regulators come in two main types: single-phase voltage and three-phase voltage. Single-phase voltage regulators are used for controlling power in single-phase electrical systems, while three-phase voltage regulators are used for three-phase systems. Single-phase voltage regulators are typically used in residential applications, while three-phase voltage regulators are commonly used in industrial settings where higher power levels are required. Both types of regulators work by controlling the amount of power flowing through the system, helping to maintain stable voltage levels and protect equipment from power surges.</p></p>
