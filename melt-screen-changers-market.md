The market can be divided into continuous, semi-continuous, and discontinuous types. Continuous screen changers involve a continuous flow of material with no interruptions, while semi-continuous screen changers allow for intermittent screen changes. Discontinuous screen changers require production to be stopped for screen replacement. There are also other types of screen changers that may have unique features or functions.</p></p>
