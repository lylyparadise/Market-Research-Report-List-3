<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS Type</li><li>APP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified bitumen membrane market is divided into two main types - SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) type and APP (Atactic Polypropylene) type. SBS membranes are known for their flexibility, elongation, and durability, making them ideal for areas with extreme weather conditions. On the other hand, APP membranes are known for their heat resistance and UV stability, making them suitable for areas with high temperatures. Both types offer excellent waterproofing properties and are widely used in the construction industry for roofing applications.</p></p>
