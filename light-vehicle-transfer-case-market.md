<p><p>Light Vehicle Transfer Case Market has two main types: Gear Driven Transfer Cases and Chain Driven Transfer Cases. Gear Driven Transfer Cases use a series of gears to transfer power from the transmission to the front and rear axles. They are known for their durability and ability to handle heavy loads. On the other hand, Chain Driven Transfer Cases use a chain to transfer power, providing smoother operation and quieter performance. Both types have their advantages and are used in different vehicles based on their specific needs.</p></p>
