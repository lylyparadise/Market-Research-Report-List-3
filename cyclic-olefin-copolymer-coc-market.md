<p><p>Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) and Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) are types of high-performance thermoplastics known for their excellent chemical resistance, low moisture absorption, and high transparency. COC is a copolymer of cycloolefin monomers, while COP is a homopolymer of cyclic olefin monomers. Both materials are used in various packaging, optical, and medical applications due to their exceptional properties. The market for COC and COP is growing due to the increasing demand for high-quality materials in industries such as healthcare, food packaging, and electronics.</p></p>
