<p><p>Diagnostic ECG market includes Resting & Stress ECG Systems and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG systems are used to record heart activity while the patient is at rest, providing crucial data on heart function. Stress ECG systems assess heart activity during physical activity or stress tests. Holter Monitors are portable devices that continuously record heart activity for 24-48 hours, offering a more comprehensive view of heart function. These types of ECG systems play a vital role in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions.</p></p>
