RO + Ion Exchange Column technology combines reverse osmosis with ion exchange to remove impurities from water. On the other hand, RO + EDI systems utilize electrodialysis to further purify water after the reverse osmosis process. There are also other types of ultrapure water equipment available in the market that use different purification methods to meet the specific requirements of various industries.</p></p>
