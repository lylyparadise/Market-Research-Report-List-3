<p><p>Industrial thermal batch furnaces come in various types, including atmosphere furnaces and vacuum furnaces. Atmosphere furnaces provide a controlled environment with specific gas compositions to prevent oxidation during the heating process. Vacuum furnaces, on the other hand, operate in a low-pressure environment to eliminate impurities and reduce contamination. Both types of furnaces are used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and metalworking for processes like annealing, brazing, and sintering to achieve precise and uniform heating results.</p></p>
