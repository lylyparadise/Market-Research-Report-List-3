<p><p>Power semiconductor module market is segmented into diodes, transistors, thyristors, and others. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, while transistors control the flow of current. Thyristors are semiconductor devices that act as switches, controlling the flow of electrical current by turning on and off. Other types of modules include insulated-gate bipolar transistors (IGBTs) and silicon-controlled rectifiers (SCRs). These different types of modules play a crucial role in various power electronics applications, such as in motor control, power supplies, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
