<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital positioners are used in various industries for precise control of valve actuators. Single Acting Positioners are designed for pneumatic actuators that only require air to move the actuator in one direction. Double Acting Positioners are used for actuators that need air pressure to move the actuator in both directions. Single Acting Positioners are simpler in design and are typically more cost-effective, while Double Acting Positioners offer more flexibility in control. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate positioning of valves in industrial processes.</p></p>
