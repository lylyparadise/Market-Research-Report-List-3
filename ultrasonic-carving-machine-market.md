<p><p>Ultrasonic carving machines are available in three main market types: CNC (Computer Numerical Control), manually-controlled, and PLC-controlled. CNC machines are highly automated and operate using computer programs to precisely control the carving process. Manually-controlled machines require operators to manually adjust settings and controls during the carving process. PLC-controlled machines use programmable logic controllers to automate and control various functions of the carving process. Each type of machine offers different levels of control and automation to suit the needs of various users.</p></p>
