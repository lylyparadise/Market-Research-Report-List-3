<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) is a cutting method used in manufacturing that involves removing material from a workpiece using electrical discharges. There are three main types of EDM market - Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM uses a shaped electrode to create the desired shape in the workpiece, Wire EDM uses a thin wire to cut through the workpiece, and Hole Drilling EDM creates holes in the workpiece using a rotating electrode. Each type of EDM has its own unique applications and benefits in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
