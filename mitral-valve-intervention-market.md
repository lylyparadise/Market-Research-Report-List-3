<p><strong>The Mitral Valve Intervention Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Replacement Product</li><li>Repair Product</li><li>Annulus Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mitral valve intervention market consists of three main types of products: replacement products, repair products, and annulus products. Replacement products involve replacing the damaged mitral valve with a prosthetic valve. Repair products help in repairing the existing mitral valve without completely replacing it. Annulus products focus on repairing or replacing the annulus, which is the ring-like structure surrounding the mitral valve. These products cater to different patient needs and offer a variety of options for managing mitral valve disorders.</p></p>
