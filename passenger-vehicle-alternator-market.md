<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Alternator Market types include Salient Pole Type and Smooth Cylindrical Type. Salient Pole Type alternators have protruding poles on the rotor, enabling easier maintenance and repair. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles. Smooth Cylindrical Type alternators have a sleek cylindrical design, providing better efficiency and compactness. They are commonly found in passenger cars and light vehicles.
