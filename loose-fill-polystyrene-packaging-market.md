<p><p>Loose Fill Polystyrene Packaging comes in different types such as Ordinary Polystyrene, Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), and Specialty Polystyrene (SPS). Ordinary Polystyrene is the most common type used for packaging due to its versatility and affordability. EPS is known for its insulation properties and lightweight nature, making it ideal for protecting fragile items during shipping. HIPS is a tougher version of polystyrene, offering enhanced impact resistance. SPS is a specialized form of polystyrene designed for specific packaging needs, such as food storage or medical supplies.</p></p>
