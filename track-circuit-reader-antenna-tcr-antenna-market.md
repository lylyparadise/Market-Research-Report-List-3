<p><p>Track Circuit Reader Antennas (TCR Antennas) are used for detecting train presence on rail tracks. There are two main types of TCR Antennas - DC and AC. DC TCR Antennas are used in direct current systems while AC TCR Antennas are used in alternating current systems. DC antennas are typically used in older railway systems where the track circuits are powered by DC voltage. AC antennas are used in newer railway systems where track circuits are powered by AC voltage. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient train operations.</p></p>
