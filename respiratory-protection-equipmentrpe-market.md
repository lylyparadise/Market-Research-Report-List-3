<p><strong>The Respiratory Protection Equipment(RPE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure APR</li><li>Positive Pressure PAPR</li><li>Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) market consists of various types of respirators to protect individuals from inhaling harmful substances. Negative Pressure Air Purifying Respirators (APR) rely on the wearer to create a negative pressure inside the mask. Positive Pressure Powered Air Purifying Respirators (PAPR) use a powered fan to deliver clean air to the wearer. Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) provide the highest level of respiratory protection by supplying the wearer with a clean air source from a compressed air tank.</p></p>
