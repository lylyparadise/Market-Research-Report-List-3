<p><p>Standard Tactile Switch Market has two main types: Surface-Mounting and Through-hole Mounting. Surface-Mounting switches are mounted directly onto the PCB, saving space and allowing for higher density layouts. Through-hole Mounting switches require holes to be drilled in the PCB for mounting, providing a more stable and robust connection. Both types are commonly used in electronic devices and appliances to provide tactile feedback to the user when pressed. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
