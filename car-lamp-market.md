<p><p>The car lamp market encompasses three main types of lighting technologies: Halogen lamps, HID lamps, and LED lamps. Halogen lamps are the most commonly used and provide a warm, yellowish light. HID lamps produce a bright, white light using a gas discharge process. LED lamps are the most energy-efficient option, lasting longer and producing a brighter light than both halogen and HID lamps. Each type of lamp offers unique benefits and caters to different consumer preferences in the automotive lighting industry.</p></p>
