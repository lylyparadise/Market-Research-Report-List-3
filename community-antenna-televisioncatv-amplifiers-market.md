<p><p>Community Antenna Television (CATV) amplifiers are used to strengthen and improve the quality of signals in cable television networks. There are two main types of CATV amplifiers: outdoor and indoor. Outdoor CATV amplifiers are installed outside and are designed to amplify signals received from the distribution network before they are distributed to individual households. Indoor CATV amplifiers, on the other hand, are installed inside buildings and are used to amplify signals within the building to ensure clear reception for each subscriber.</p></p>
