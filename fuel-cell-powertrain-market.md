<p><p>The Fuel Cell Powertrain market can be categorized into Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD), Front-Wheel Drive (FWD), and All-Wheel Drive (AWD) types based on the distribution of power to the wheels. </p><p>Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD) vehicles have power transmitted to the rear wheels, providing a traditional driving experience with better weight distribution. Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) vehicles have power sent to the front wheels, offering better traction in slippery conditions. All-Wheel Drive (AWD) vehicles distribute power to all four wheels, enhancing traction and handling in various road conditions.</p></p>
