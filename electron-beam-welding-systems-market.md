<p><p>Electron beam welding systems are classified into two main types: stationary welding torch and mobile welding torch. Stationary welding torch systems are typically used for welding components that are fixed in one position, providing greater stability and precision in the welding process. On the other hand, mobile welding torch systems are designed for welding applications that require flexibility and maneuverability, allowing the torch to be moved easily to different locations for welding various components. Both types of systems play a crucial role in different welding applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
