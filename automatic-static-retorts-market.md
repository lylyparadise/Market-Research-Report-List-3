<p><p>Automatic static retorts are used in food processing to sterilize and preserve products in sealed containers. The main types of automatic static retorts are the Steam and Water Spray Retort, Water Immersion Retort, and Air Steam Retort. </p><p>In the Steam and Water Spray Retort, products are sterilized using a combination of steam and hot water spray. The Water Immersion Retort involves submerging the products in hot water for sterilization. In the Air Steam Retort, products are sterilized using a combination of steam and hot air circulation. Each type offers different benefits and is suitable for different products and processes.</p></p>
