<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board Inspection Equipment market includes various types of equipment, with two common categories being Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI). AOI systems use cameras and software to detect defects in PCBs such as missing components or misalignments, offering high accuracy and efficiency. On the other hand, AXI systems utilize X-ray technology to inspect hidden defects in PCBs, providing detailed images for thorough analysis. Both types of inspection equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of PCBs in electronic manufacturing processes.</p></p>
