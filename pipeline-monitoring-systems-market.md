<p><p>Pipeline monitoring systems come in two main types: metallic and non-metallic. Metallic pipeline monitoring systems are typically used with metal pipelines and utilize technologies such as ultrasonic testing or electromagnetic inspection to detect flaws or corrosion. On the other hand, non-metallic pipeline monitoring systems are designed for pipelines made of materials like plastic or composites, and rely on methods such as acoustic emission monitoring or fiber optic sensing to identify any potential issues. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and reliability of pipelines.</p></p>
