<p><p>Pharmaceutical Grade Glucose is available in two main types - API Grade and Excipient Grade. API Grade refers to glucose that is used as an active pharmaceutical ingredient in the formulation of drugs, ensuring high purity and quality standards. Excipient Grade, on the other hand, refers to the glucose used as an inert substance in pharmaceutical products to give bulk or stability to the formulation. Both API Grade and Excipient Grade are crucial in the pharmaceutical industry for ensuring the safety and efficacy of medications.</p></p>
