<p><p>Milling correctors market can be divided into four main types. Hydrate correctors are used to adjust the hydration level of materials for improved milling performance. Tolerance correctors ensure precise milling by adjusting for any deviations in material dimensions. Flexible correctors provide versatility in milling operations by allowing for quick adjustments to meet varying requirements. Adhesive force correctors are used to regulate the stickiness of materials during milling for consistent results. Each type of corrector plays a crucial role in optimizing the milling process for different materials and applications.</p></p>
