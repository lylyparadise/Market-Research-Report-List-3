<p><p>Automotive leaf spring suspension systems are categorized into two main types: multi-leaf spring and mono-leaf spring. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of spring leaves stacked on top of each other, providing greater load-carrying capacity and stability. On the other hand, mono-leaf springs consist of a single, thicker spring leaf that offers a smoother ride and improved handling. Both types of leaf spring suspensions are widely used in various vehicles, with each having its own strengths and advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
