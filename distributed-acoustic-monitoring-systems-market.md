<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Monitoring</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Monitoring Systems utilize different methods to monitor acoustic signals in various environments. Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Monitoring focuses on the detection of acoustic waves that travel parallel to the direction of energy propagation, providing detailed information on the source of the sound. Transverse Wave Acoustic Monitoring, on the other hand, focuses on detecting waves that travel perpendicular to the direction of energy propagation, offering insights into the structural integrity of materials. Both types of monitoring play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of industrial operations.</p></p>
