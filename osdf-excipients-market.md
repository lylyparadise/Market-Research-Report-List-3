<p><p>OSDF (Oral Solid Dosage Forms) Excipients are essential components used in the formulation of pharmaceutical products. These excipients are available in various market types, including Powder, Granules, Tablet, Capsule, and Dropping Pill. Powder excipients are used for direct compression or wet granulation process. Granules are pre-processed particles used in the manufacturing of tablets and capsules. Tablets are solid dosage forms made by compressing powdered ingredients. Capsules provide a convenient way to deliver drugs in a solid form. Dropping pills are small solid dosage forms that dissolve in the mouth for quick absorption.</p></p>
