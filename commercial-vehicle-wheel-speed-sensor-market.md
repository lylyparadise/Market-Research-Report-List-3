<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensors are of two types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a semiconductor device to detect changes in magnetic fields generated by the rotating wheel, providing accurate speed readings. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors use a magnet and coil system to detect wheel speed. Both types of sensors are widely used in commercial vehicles to provide crucial data for vehicle stability control systems, ABS, and traction control systems.</p></p>
