<p><p>Wireless Pick to Light systems are used in two main market types: Manual and Auto Guided. In the Manual market, workers manually pick items from warehouse shelves based on the light signals provided by the system. This type of system is ideal for smaller warehouses with simple picking operations. On the other hand, in the Auto Guided market, automated vehicles or robots are used to transport items to picking stations, where workers then pick the items based on the pick to light signals. This type is suitable for larger warehouses with complex picking operations.</p></p>
