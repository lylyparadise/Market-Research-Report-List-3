<p><p>Dynamic Water Cutting Machines can be classified into two main types: Sandless Cutting and Abrasive Cutting markets. Sandless cutting machines use high-pressure water jets to cut through materials without the need for abrasive particles, resulting in cleaner cuts with no abrasive residue. Abrasive cutting machines, on the other hand, use a mixture of water and abrasive particles to cut through tough materials such as metal and stone, providing more precise and efficient cutting abilities. Both markets cater to different cutting requirements and industries.</p></p>
