<p><p>Electric food steamers come in two main types: pressure electric food steamers and pressureless electric food steamers. Pressure electric food steamers use built-up steam pressure to cook food quickly, while pressureless electric food steamers rely on gentle heat and steam to cook food more slowly. Pressure steamers are ideal for cooking tough meats or large quantities of food in less time, while pressureless steamers are great for delicate foods like vegetables or fish that require a more gentle cooking process. Both types offer efficient and convenient ways to steam food.</p></p>
