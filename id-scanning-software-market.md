<p><p>ID scanning software is available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. Cloud-Based software, on the other hand, is hosted and maintained by a third-party provider, offering accessibility from any location with an internet connection. Cloud-Based software typically involves lower upfront costs and easier scalability, while On-Premise software gives users more control over their data and security measures.</p></p>
