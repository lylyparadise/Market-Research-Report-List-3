<p><p>Rotavator blades are categorized into three main types based on their shape and function: L blades, C blades, and J blades. L blades are designed with a single curve that helps in cutting and pulverizing the soil effectively. C blades have a curved shape resembling the letter "C" which allows for better cutting and mixing of the soil. J blades have a hook-like shape that helps in lifting and turning the soil more efficiently. Each type of blade serves a specific purpose in agricultural machinery for optimal soil preparation.</p></p>
