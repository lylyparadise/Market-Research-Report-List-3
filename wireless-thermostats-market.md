<p><p>Wireless thermostats come in two main types - Battery-powered and Hardwired. Battery-powered wireless thermostats are easy to install and do not require extensive wiring, making them a convenient choice for homeowners. They are powered by batteries, which need to be replaced periodically. On the other hand, Hardwired wireless thermostats are connected directly to the home's electrical system, providing a continuous power source. They are typically more reliable and offer additional features compared to battery-powered models.</p></p>
