<p><p>Conductive Carbon Blacks are categorized into four main types: CC (conductive carbon black), CF (conductive furnace black), SCF (superconductive furnace black), and XCF (extra conductive furnace black). CC is considered fairly conductive, CF is moderately conductive, SCF is highly conductive, and XCF is extremely conductive. These different types cater to various industries and applications where levels of conductivity required may vary. Overall, Conductive Carbon Blacks play a crucial role in enhancing the electrical and thermal conductivity of different materials.</p></p>
