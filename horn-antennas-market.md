<p><p>Horn antennas are categorized into three main types based on their design and construction. Twistable waveguide horn antennas feature a twistable waveguide structure that allows for easy adjustment of the beamwidth. Seamless non-twistable horn antennas are designed without any twisting mechanism, offering a fixed beamwidth. Non-twistable horn antennas provide a stable and reliable option for applications requiring a constant beamwidth. Each type caters to specific market needs and preferences, offering unique advantages in terms of flexibility, stability, and performance.</p></p>
