<p><p>The Alzheimer's Therapeutics Market is segmented into three main types: Donepezil, Memantine, and Rivastigmine. Donepezil is a cholinesterase inhibitor that helps improve cognitive function. Memantine works by regulating glutamate activity in the brain, helping to slow the progression of symptoms. Rivastigmine is another cholinesterase inhibitor that can improve cognition and daily functioning in individuals with Alzheimer's disease. These medications are commonly used in the treatment of Alzheimer's to help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.</p></p>
