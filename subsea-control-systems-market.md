Topside control systems are located on the production platform above the water surface, while underwater control systems are situated on the seabed close to the subsea production equipment. Both types play a critical role in monitoring and managing the operation of subsea oil and gas production systems, ensuring efficient and safe production processes. These systems are essential for maintaining control and integrity in offshore operations.</p></p>
