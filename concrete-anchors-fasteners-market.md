<p><ul><li>Mechanical Anchors or Fasteners</li><li>Adhesive Anchors or Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete anchors fasteners market includes two main types - mechanical anchors or fasteners, and adhesive anchors or fasteners. Mechanical anchors or fasteners are installed by inserting a bolt into a hole drilled in the concrete and tightening a nut to secure the connection. Adhesive anchors or fasteners, on the other hand, use a chemical adhesive to bond the anchor to the concrete. Both types offer strong and reliable connections for a variety of applications in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
