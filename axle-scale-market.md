<p><ul><li>Axle Scale</li><li>Wheel Scale</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axle scales and wheel scales are both types of scales used to weigh vehicles and their cargo. Axle scales are specifically designed to weigh individual axles of vehicles, providing accurate weight measurements for each axle. This information is helpful in determining weight distribution and ensuring vehicles are within legal weight limits. Wheel scales, on the other hand, are used to weigh entire vehicles by measuring the weight placed on each wheel. Both types of scales are essential in various industries such as transportation, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
