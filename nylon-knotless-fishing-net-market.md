<p><p>Nylon knotless fishing nets are commonly categorized into two market types: Depthway and Lengthway. Depthway nets are designed to catch fish in the water column by hanging vertically in the water. These nets are suitable for catching fish that swim at various depths. On the other hand, Lengthway nets are set horizontally in the water to catch fish that swim parallel to the net. These nets are ideal for trapping fish that move lengthwise along the net. Both market types play a significant role in the fishing industry.</p></p>
