<p><p>One-component RTV rubber is categorized into two main types - Addition Type and Condensation Type. Addition Type RTV rubber cures through an addition reaction where a curing agent is added to initiate cross-linking of the polymer chains. On the other hand, Condensation Type RTV rubber cures through a condensation reaction where curing agent is released as a byproduct during the curing process. Both types have specific applications and properties that make them suitable for different industries and uses.</p></p>
