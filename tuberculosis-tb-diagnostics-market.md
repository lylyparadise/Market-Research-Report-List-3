<p><p>Tuberculosis (TB) diagnostics market includes various types of methods such as culture-based diagnostics, sputum smear microscopy, rapid molecular diagnostics, and other emerging technologies. Culture-based diagnostics involve isolating and growing the bacteria in a laboratory setting. Sputum smear microscopy identifies TB bacteria under a microscope. Rapid molecular diagnostics use genetic material to detect the presence of TB quickly. Other methods include serological tests and nucleic acid amplification tests. Each type of diagnostic test has its own advantages and limitations in detecting TB infection.</p></p>
