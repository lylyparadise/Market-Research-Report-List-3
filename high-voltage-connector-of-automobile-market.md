<p><p>The High Voltage Connector of Automobile Market can be categorized into three types: Line to Line, Line to Board, and Board to Board. Line to Line connectors are used to connect two cables or wires together, Line to Board connectors are designed to connect cables to a printed circuit board, and Board to Board connectors are used to connect two PCBs together. These different types of connectors play a crucial role in ensuring proper electrical connectivity within an automobile's high voltage system.</p></p>
