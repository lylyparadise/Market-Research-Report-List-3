<p><p>The Automotive Telematics System market consists of three main types: Embedded Type, Tethered Type, and Integrated Type. Embedded Type systems are built directly into the vehicle and provide continuous tracking and monitoring capabilities. Tethered Type systems rely on external devices, such as smartphones, for connectivity and data transmission. Integrated Type systems combine both embedded and tethered technologies to offer seamless connectivity and enhanced features. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
