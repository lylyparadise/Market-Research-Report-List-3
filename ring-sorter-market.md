<p><p>Ring Sorter is a type of automated sorting system used in distribution centers to efficiently sort packages or items based on their destination. There are three main types of Ring Sorter in the market - Toroidal Cross Belt, Bag Sorter, and Tilting Plate Sorter. Toroidal Cross Belt uses a looped belt to carry items along a circular path for sorting. Bag Sorter uses individual bags or pouches to carry items to their designated chutes. Tilting Plate Sorter tilts plates to push items into chutes for sorting.</p></p>
