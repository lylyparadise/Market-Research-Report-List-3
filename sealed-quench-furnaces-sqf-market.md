<p><strong>The Sealed Quench Furnaces (SQF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Through Type</li><li>In-Out Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealed Quench Furnaces (SQF) are commonly categorized into two main types based on their operation process - Straight Through Type and In-Out Type. In Straight Through Type furnaces, the loading and unloading of the components are done from the same end of the furnace, efficient for continuous production. In contrast, In-Out Type furnaces have separate loading and unloading chambers, allowing for simultaneous heating and cooling of components. Both types cater to different industrial needs and processes in the heat treatment industry.</p></p>
