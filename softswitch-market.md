<p><p>Class 4 and Class 5 softswitches are two main types of softswitches in the market. Class 4 softswitches are primarily used for routing and transferring VoIP traffic between carriers, making them suitable for wholesale VoIP service providers. On the other hand, Class 5 softswitches are used for connecting end-users to the network, making them ideal for retail VoIP service providers. Both types of softswitches play a crucial role in the VoIP industry, with each serving a specific purpose in the communication ecosystem.</p></p>
