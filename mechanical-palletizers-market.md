<p><p>Mechanical palletizers are available in two main types: low level and high level. Low level palletizers are suitable for smaller production lines, where palletizing is done at ground level to minimize manual handling and reduce operator strain. On the other hand, high level palletizers are designed for larger production lines, where palletizing is done at a higher level to increase efficiency and throughput. Both types of palletizers offer automation and can handle a variety of products and packaging formats.</p></p>
