<p><p>Parallel Type Injection Molding Machine is a type of machine that has two independent injection units working simultaneously, allowing for the use of two different colors or materials in one part. Vertical Type Injection Molding Machine, on the other hand, has one injection unit but can still produce two-color parts by rotating the mold or using a rotary table. Both types of machines are suitable for producing products with intricate designs and multiple colors, offering versatility and efficiency in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
