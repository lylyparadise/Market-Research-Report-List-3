<p><p>Copter market types include single-rotor, coaxial twin rotor, and split shaft twin rotor designs. Single-rotor copters have one main rotor and a tail rotor for stability. Coaxial twin rotor copters have two rotors mounted on top of each other to cancel out rotational forces.
