<p><ul><li>Ink Cartridges</li><li>Toner Cartridges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ink cartridges are used in inkjet printers and contain liquid ink that is sprayed onto the paper during printing. They are commonly used for home and office printers. Toner cartridges, on the other hand, are used in laser printers and contain a fine powder that is fused onto the paper using heat and pressure. They are more commonly used in professional settings due to their ability to produce high-quality prints at a faster speed. Both markets are constantly evolving with advancements in technology and increasing demands for efficient and cost-effective printing solutions.</p></p>
