<p><strong>The Aircraft Airframe Fuel Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Injection</li><li>Pump Feed Technology</li><li>Gravity Feed Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Airframe Fuel Systems market includes three main types: Fuel Injection, Pump Feed Technology, and Gravity Feed Technology. Fuel Injection systems deliver fuel directly to the engine cylinders, providing precise fuel delivery and efficiency. Pump Feed Technology utilizes fuel pumps to deliver fuel from the tank to the engine, ensuring a consistent flow of fuel. Gravity Feed Technology relies on the force of gravity to move fuel from the tank to the engine, making it a simpler and more cost-effective option for some aircraft.</p></p>
