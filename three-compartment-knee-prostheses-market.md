<p><p>Cement Fixation knee prostheses require the use of bone cement to attach the components to the existing bone, providing stability and long-term fixation. Cement Alternatives knee prostheses utilize various techniques such as press-fit and porous coatings to achieve fixation without the need for cement. Each type of knee prosthesis has its advantages and disadvantages, with cement fixation offering immediate stability but potential long-term concerns, and cement alternatives providing a more modern approach with potential for improved long-term outcomes.</p></p>
