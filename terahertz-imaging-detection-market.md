Passive Terahertz imaging operates by detecting natural Terahertz radiation emitted by objects, while active Terahertz imaging involves the use of an external source to generate Terahertz radiation for imaging. Passive Terahertz imaging is commonly used for security screening purposes, while active Terahertz imaging is often utilized in scientific research and medical imaging applications. Both types play a crucial role in advancing imaging technology for various industries.</p></p>
