<p><p>Agriculture bio stimulants are categorized into three main types: acid-based, extract-based, and other. Acid-based bio stimulants are derived from organic acids and help improve plant nutrient uptake and overall health. Extract-based bio stimulants are made from plant extracts and help promote growth, yield, and stress resistance in plants. The "other" category may include bio stimulants that do not fall into the acid or extract-based categories, such as microbial or seaweed-based products. Each type of bio stimulant offers unique benefits for agriculture and crop production.</p></p>
