<p><ul><li>Double Pipe System</li><li>Three Pipe System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packaged refrigeration systems are available in various types, including double pipe systems and three pipe systems. The double pipe system consists of two separate pipes for the refrigerant, one for the liquid and the other for the vapor. On the other hand, the three pipe system includes an additional pipe for the oil separator, which helps in ensuring efficient operation and maintenance of the refrigeration system. Both types offer distinct advantages in terms of performance, energy efficiency, and overall functionality in different applications.</p></p>
