<p><strong>The Digital Pathology Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Telepathology</li><li>Dynamic Telepathology</li><li>Hybrid Telepathology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Pathology Systems Market includes three main types: Static Telepathology, Dynamic Telepathology, and Hybrid Telepathology. Static Telepathology involves capturing and storing images for later review. Dynamic Telepathology allows real-time consultation and collaboration between pathologists. Hybrid Telepathology combines both static and dynamic methods for a comprehensive approach. These systems enable pathologists to easily access and share digital slides, improving efficiency and accuracy in diagnosing and treating patients. The market for Digital Pathology Systems continues to grow as healthcare institutions adopt these advanced technologies.</p></p>
