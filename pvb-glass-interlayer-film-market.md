<p><p>PVB Glass Interlayer Film comes in two main types: Standard Polyvinyl Butyral and Structural Polyvinyl Butyral. Standard PVB is commonly used in automotive and architectural applications to provide safety and sound insulation. It is known for its high transparency and adhesive properties. On the other hand, Structural PVB is designed for use in high-performance applications where strength and durability are critical, such as in hurricane-resistant windows and glass facades. Both types offer varying levels of security, UV protection, and impact resistance to enhance the safety and durability of glass installations.</p></p>
