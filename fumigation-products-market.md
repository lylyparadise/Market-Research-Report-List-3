<p><p>Fumigation products are classified into three main types based on their physical state: solid, liquid, and gas. Solid fumigation products typically come in the form of pellets, powder, or tablets that release fumes when exposed to air. Liquid fumigation products are solutions or emulsions that can be sprayed or applied directly to surfaces. Gas fumigation products are released into the air as vapors or aerosols to penetrate and kill pests in enclosed spaces. Each type has specific applications and effectiveness in pest control.</p></p>
