<p><p>Packet & Parcel Sorting System market can be categorized into various types such as Cross Belt Sorter, Shoe Sorter, Tilt Tray Sorter, and Others. Cross Belt Sorter system uses conveyor belts to move items to designated bins for sorting. Shoe Sorter system uses a series of shoes that divert packages into different chutes based on predetermined criteria. Tilt Tray Sorter system utilizes trays that tilt to slide packages into the appropriate chute. Other sorting systems may include technologies like robotic arms or automated guided vehicles for efficient sorting processes.</p></p>
