<p><p>The polycarbonate panel market consists of three main types: Multi-wall panels, Corrugated panels, and Solid panels. Multi-wall panels are constructed with multiple layers for added strength and insulation. Corrugated panels are designed with ridges for improved strength and durability. Solid panels are made from a single layer of polycarbonate for a high level of clarity and impact resistance. Each type caters to different applications and requirements within the polycarbonate panel market.</p></p>
