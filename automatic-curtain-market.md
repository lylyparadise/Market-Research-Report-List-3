<p><p>The automatic curtain market is divided into two main types: Ripplefold and Pinch Pleat. Ripplefold curtains have a simple yet elegant design, with evenly spaced ripples along the top of the fabric. They provide a clean and modern look to any room. On the other hand, Pinch Pleat curtains have a more traditional and formal appearance, featuring pleats pinched together at the top for a classic and sophisticated style. Both types offer convenient automation options for opening and closing, adding convenience and luxury to any space.</p></p>
