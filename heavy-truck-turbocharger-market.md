<p><p>The heavy truck turbocharger market is divided into two main types: mono turbo and twin turbo. Mono turbo systems consist of a single turbocharger that increases engine power and efficiency. On the other hand, twin turbo systems utilize two turbochargers for even more power and performance. Twin turbo setups typically involve a smaller turbocharger for quicker response at lower engine speeds, and a larger turbocharger for higher power output at higher speeds.Both types offer advantages in terms of performance and efficiency for heavy trucks.</p></p>
