<p><strong>The Lead-Acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lead-Acid Battery Market includes two main types: VRLA (valve-regulated lead-acid) Battery and Flooded Battery. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, suitable for applications where minimal maintenance is desired. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance and watering due to their open design. VRLA batteries are more expensive but offer higher efficiency and longer lifespan, while flooded batteries are cheaper but require more maintenance. Both types cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
