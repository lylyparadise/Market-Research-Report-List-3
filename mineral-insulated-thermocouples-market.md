<p><strong>The Mineral Insulated Thermocouples Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral insulated thermocouples are available in three main types based on their construction: Grounded type, Ungrounded type, and Exposed type. Grounded type thermocouples have the thermocouple wires welded to the sheath, providing good thermal contact for accurate measurements. Ungrounded type thermocouples have isolated wires for electrical insulation, reducing the risk of electrical interference. Exposed type thermocouples have the thermocouple junction directly exposed to the measurement environment for faster response times. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various industrial applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
