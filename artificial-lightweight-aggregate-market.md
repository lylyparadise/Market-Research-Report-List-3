<p><p>The Artificial Lightweight Aggregate market consists of various types such as Expanded Clay, Expanded Shale and Slate, Sintered Fly Ash, Stone Sludge, Expanded Perlite, and Others. Expanded Clay is produced by heating clay in a rotary kiln, while Expanded Shale and Slate are created by expanding shale or slate in a kiln. Sintered Fly Ash is made by heating fly ash pellets, and Stone Sludge is created from waste stone materials. Expanded Perlite is produced by heating perlite. Other types include innovative lightweight aggregates developed from different materials.</p></p>
