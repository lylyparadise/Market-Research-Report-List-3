<p><p>Online meeting software can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access meetings from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed and managed within an organization's own network, providing greater control over security and data privacy. Cloud-based solutions are popular for their flexibility and scalability, while on-premises solutions are favored by organizations with specific security or compliance requirements.</p></p>
