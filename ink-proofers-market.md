<p><p>Ink proofers are devices used in the printing industry to apply a controlled amount of ink onto a substrate for testing purposes. There are two main types of ink proofers: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic proofers require some manual intervention for loading substrates and adjusting settings, while fully automatic proofers operate without the need for constant human intervention, performing tasks such as feeding substrates and adjusting ink levels automatically. Both types of proofers offer efficient and accurate ink testing capabilities for printing applications.</p></p>
