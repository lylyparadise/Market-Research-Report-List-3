<p><p>Fully automatic vacuum lamination equipment is designed to operate independently and carry out all lamination processes without human intervention. It requires minimal manual input and is ideal for high-volume production. On the other hand, semi-automatic vacuum lamination equipment requires some level of human intervention to operate, such as loading and unloading materials.
