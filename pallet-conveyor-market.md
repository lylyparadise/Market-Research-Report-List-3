<p><p>Pallet conveyor market offers various types catering to different needs. Drag chain conveyors are ideal for heavy-duty applications, while roller conveyors can be either driven or gravity-operated, providing flexibility and efficiency. Plastic type conveyors are lightweight and durable, suitable for clean environments. Slat conveyors are designed for handling irregularly shaped or heavy loads. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in a wide range of industries to streamline material handling processes.</p></p>
