<p><p>Biological sealants are medical products used in surgical procedures to seal tissues and prevent leakage of fluids. The market is categorized into different types based on the materials used in the sealants. These include fibrin sealants, collagen-based sealants, and others such as albumin-based sealants. Fibrin sealants are made from fibrinogen and thrombin and are commonly used in surgeries for hemostasis and tissue bonding. Collagen-based sealants are derived from collagen proteins and are effective in wound closure and tissue repair. Other sealants may include different formulations for specific surgical applications.</p></p>
