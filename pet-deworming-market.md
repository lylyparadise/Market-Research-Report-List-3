<p><p>Pet deworming market includes internal deworming products and external deworming products. Internal deworming involves medications that target internal parasites such as roundworms, hookworms, and tapeworms. External deworming products focus on treating external parasites like fleas, ticks, and mites. Both markets cater to pet owners looking to maintain their pets' health and prevent infestations. Internal deworming products are usually administered orally, while external deworming products come in the form of shampoos, collars, or topical treatments.</p></p>
