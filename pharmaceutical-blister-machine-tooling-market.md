Seal tools are essential for creating airtight seals on the packaging. Guide tracks/rails help in the smooth movement of the blister packs through the machine. Perforation & scoring dies are used to create easy-to-open features on the packaging. Other tools may include embossing tools or cutting blades for additional customization of the blister packs.</p></p>
