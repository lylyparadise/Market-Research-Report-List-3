<p><p>Gears market comprises various types such as Spur Gear, Helical Gear, Bevel Gear, Worm Gear, Gear Rack and Others. Spur gears have straight teeth parallel to the axis, while helical gears have angled teeth for smoother operation. Bevel gears have cone-shaped teeth for intersecting shafts, and worm gears have a screw-like mechanism for high torque applications. Gear racks are straight toothed bars used with spur gears. Other types include planetary gears, internal gears, and crown gears for specific gear arrangements.</p></p>
