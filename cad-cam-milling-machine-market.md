<p><strong>The CAD CAM Milling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>5-axis</li><li>4-axis</li><li>3-axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CAD CAM milling machines are classified based on the number of axes they operate on. 3-axis machines move on three axes: X, Y, and Z, making them suitable for basic milling tasks. 4-axis machines add an extra rotary axis, allowing for more complex and intricate designs. 5-axis machines have two extra rotary axes, offering the highest level of precision and flexibility for intricate and curved designs. Each type of machine caters to different levels of complexity and detail in the milling process.</p></p>
