<p><p>Semiconductor mold cleaners are essential for the maintenance of semiconductor manufacturing equipment. Two common types of mold cleaners are melamine-based mold cleaners and rubber-based mold cleaners. Melamine-based mold cleaners are effective for removing tough residues and contaminants from mold surfaces. On the other hand, rubber-based mold cleaners are gentle on delicate mold surfaces and can effectively remove organic substances. Both types of mold cleaners play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning and longevity of semiconductor molds in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
