<p><ul><li>Cooled Infrared Imaging</li><li>Uncooled Infrared Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared imaging technology is classified into two main types: cooled and uncooled infrared imaging. Cooled infrared imaging systems require a cooling mechanism to maintain the temperature of the infrared detector, resulting in higher sensitivity and better image quality. On the other hand, uncooled infrared imaging systems do not require cooling, making them more compact, lightweight, and less expensive. Both types of infrared imaging have their own advantages and applications, catering to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
