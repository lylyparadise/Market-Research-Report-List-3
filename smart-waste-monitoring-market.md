<p><p>Smart waste monitoring uses various technologies to collect real-time data on waste levels in containers. Ultrasound sensors measure waste levels by emitting sound waves and analyzing the reflected signal. Infrared sensors detect waste levels by analyzing the heat emitted from the waste. Other technologies include weight sensors, fill level sensors, and camera-based systems. Each type of market technology offers different advantages in terms of accuracy, cost-effectiveness, and ease of implementation for waste management companies.</p></p>
