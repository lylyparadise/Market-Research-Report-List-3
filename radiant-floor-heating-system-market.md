<p><p>Radiant floor heating systems can be installed in three main types of markets based on the placement of the heating elements. Installation above wooden floor involves laying the heating system directly on top of the existing wooden flooring. Installation under subfloor requires placing the heating system beneath the subfloor for more efficient heat distribution. Ordinary concrete slab or underground installation involves embedding the heating system within a concrete slab or underground for effective heating of the space. Each market type offers unique benefits in terms of energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
