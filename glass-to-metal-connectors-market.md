<p><strong>The Glass-to-Metal Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Matched Connectors/Seals</li><li>Compression Connectors/Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Matched connectors/seals are designed with materials that have similar coefficients of thermal expansion to ensure a tight and reliable seal between the glass and metal components. Compression connectors/seals use a compression force to create a seal between the glass and metal parts, providing a secure and leak-proof connection. Both types of connectors are essential in applications where high temperature and pressure differentials are present, such as in aerospace, automotive, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
