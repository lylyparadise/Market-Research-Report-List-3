<p><p>Scleritis is a rare inflammatory condition of the sclera, which can be categorized into different types based on the location and severity of the inflammation. Diffuse Anterior Scleritis involves widespread inflammation in the front of the eye. Nodular Anterior Scleritis presents as localized nodules on the anterior surface. Necrotizing Anterior Scleritis is a severe form that can lead to necrosis of the tissue. Posterior Scleritis affects the back of the eye and is less common but can cause serious complications. Treatment varies depending on the type and severity of the condition.</p></p>
